What is HSC

Hospital scheduling system is automated generator of fair and optimum schedule for all nursing staff of hospital . It uses linear optimization process to generate schedule for all wards of hopistal based on individual ward/department needs.

All staff resources which do not meet certification and qualification requirement do not get scheduled for a particular job and staff on scheduled vacation/leaves is also skipped while generating weekly schedule. Current schedule is generated for one week assuming three 8 hours shifts per day
